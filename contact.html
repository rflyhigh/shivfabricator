Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common inquiries about our services

What areas do you serve?

We primarily serve Maharashtra and neighboring states. However, for larger projects, we can work across India. Contact us with your project location for specific information.

How do I request a quote for my project?

You can request a quote by filling out our contact form, calling us directly, or sending an email with your project details. We'll get back to you within 24-48 hours with an initial estimate.

Do you provide installation services as well as fabrication?

Yes, we provide comprehensive services including design, fabrication, installation, testing, and commissioning. We can handle all aspects of your project from start to finish.

What types of industries do you serve?

We serve a wide range of industries including food processing, dairy, pharmaceuticals, chemicals, beverages, and general manufacturing. Our expertise allows us to adapt to various industrial requirements.

Do you offer maintenance services for existing installations?

Yes, we offer maintenance, repair, and upgrade services for existing installations. We can help extend the life of your equipment and improve its performance through regular maintenance.
