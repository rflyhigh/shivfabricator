Our Services

Comprehensive industrial fabrication solutions tailored to your needs

At Shiva Fabrications, we offer a wide range of industrial fabrication services designed to meet the diverse needs of our clients. With our expertise and experience, we ensure high-quality workmanship, adherence to safety standards, and timely project completion.

Food Plants

We specialize in the fabrication and installation of food processing plants, ensuring compliance with hygiene standards and industry regulations. Our comprehensive solutions include:

  • Process equipment installation
  • Sanitary piping systems
  • Storage tanks and vessels
  • Conveyor systems
  • Complete plant setup and commissioning

Dairy Plants

Our dairy plant solutions are designed to optimize production processes while maintaining the highest standards of hygiene and product quality. Our services include:

  • Pasteurization equipment installation
  • Milk processing systems
  • CIP (Clean-in-Place) systems
  • Storage and packaging solutions
  • Automation and control systems

Industrial Piping

We provide comprehensive piping solutions for various industrial applications, ensuring efficient fluid transport and system integrity. Our expertise includes:

  • GI (Galvanized Iron) Piping
  • SS (Stainless Steel) Piping
  • MS (Mild Steel) Piping
  • Process piping for various industries
  • High-pressure piping systems
  • Insulated piping solutions

Welding Services

Our team of skilled welders delivers precision welding services for a wide range of applications, ensuring structural integrity and longevity. Our welding capabilities include:

  • Argon Welding (TIG Welding)
  • MIG Welding
  • Arc Welding
  • Spot Welding
  • Specialized welding for various metals
  • Orbital welding for high-purity applications

Manpower Supply

We provide skilled and experienced manpower for industrial projects, ensuring timely completion and quality workmanship. Our manpower solutions include:

  • Skilled welders and fitters
  • Supervisors and project managers
  • Helpers and support staff
  • Specialized technicians
  • On-site training and supervision

Visit our Manpower page for detailed information on rates and terms.

Custom Fabrication

We offer custom fabrication services to meet specific client requirements, providing tailored solutions for unique industrial challenges. Our capabilities include:

  • Custom equipment fabrication
  • Structural steel fabrication
  • Sheet metal fabrication
  • Prototype development
  • Modifications and upgrades to existing systems
