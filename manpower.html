Manpower Supply

Skilled professionals for your industrial projects

At Shiva Fabrications, we provide highly skilled manpower for industrial projects of all sizes. Our workforce consists of experienced professionals who are trained in various aspects of industrial fabrication, installation, and maintenance. We ensure that all our workers are thoroughly vetted, skilled in their respective trades, and committed to maintaining the highest standards of quality and safety.

Our Workforce Categories

Skilled professionals to meet your project requirements

Supervisors

Experienced project supervisors who oversee operations, ensure quality control, and coordinate with project stakeholders.

  • Project planning and execution
  • Quality assurance
  • Team coordination
  • Safety compliance

Welders

Skilled welders proficient in various welding techniques, including argon welding for specialized applications.

  • Argon (TIG) welding
  • MIG welding
  • Arc welding
  • Pipe welding

Fitters

Experienced pipe fitters who specialize in the installation and maintenance of various piping systems.

  • GI pipe fitting
  • SS pipe fitting
  • MS pipe fitting
  • System testing

Helpers

Support staff who assist skilled workers in various tasks, ensuring smooth operations and timely completion.

  • Material handling
  • Equipment setup
  • Site preparation
  • General assistance
