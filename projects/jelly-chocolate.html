Jelly Chocolate Process Plant

Completed: 2022 Location: Maharashtra Category: Food Plant

Project Overview

The Jelly Chocolate Process Plant project involved the complete design, fabrication, and installation of a state-of-the-art processing facility for a leading confectionery manufacturer. The scope included setting up the entire production line from raw material handling to final product processing, with a focus on maintaining the highest standards of hygiene and product quality.

Scope of Work

  • Design and engineering of the complete process plant
  • Fabrication and installation of processing equipment
  • Installation of stainless steel piping system using argon welding for high hygiene standards
  • Setup of heating and cooling systems
  • Installation of control systems and automation
  • Testing and commissioning of the complete plant
  • Operator training and handover

Challenges & Solutions

Challenge: Maintaining Hygiene Standards

The food industry demands the highest levels of hygiene in all equipment and piping systems.

Solution:

We utilized high-quality stainless steel materials and argon welding techniques to ensure smooth, crevice-free joints that prevent bacterial growth. All equipment was designed with CIP (Clean-in-Place) capabilities for thorough cleaning.

Challenge: Temperature Control

Chocolate processing requires precise temperature control throughout the production line.

Solution:

We implemented advanced heating and cooling systems with precise temperature sensors and controllers to maintain optimal temperatures at each stage of the process.

Challenge: Integration with Existing Systems

The new plant needed to integrate seamlessly with the client's existing production facilities.

Solution:

Our team conducted thorough analysis of existing systems and designed compatible interfaces, ensuring smooth integration and minimal disruption to ongoing operations.

Results & Benefits

  • Increased production capacity by 40%
  • Improved product quality and consistency
  • Enhanced energy efficiency, reducing operational costs
  • Reduced maintenance requirements due to quality fabrication
  • Compliance with international food safety standards
  • Completed project ahead of schedule, allowing early production start
